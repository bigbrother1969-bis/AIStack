Plot : I want to describe the resilience and intelligence of a very disabled woman in front of her daily struggles and how she is able to overcome them. 

Main character : Alia is a 25 years old strikingly beautiful black quadriplegic woman. 
When she was 14, Alia was the victim of a drunk driver that severed her spinal cord at C4 level. 
Alia has no feeling or movement under her injury level. 
Alia is busty, slim, and tall.
Her arms and hands are thin and always stretched flat on the ergonomic armrests of her powerchair.
Her legs are atrophied and pressed tightly together because of the paralysis, with an urine bag stick to one leg.
Alia has an benevolent and happy attitude, with an eternal mischievous smile. 
Alia has very stylish snow white hair, piercing green eyes, sophisticated makeup, manicured nails and dark skin. 
Alia wears generally very stylish outfits specially adapted to her disability. 
Alia wears stylish earings and necklace. She also wears stylish glasses with coloured lenses when she works or reads. 
She usually wears designer high-heeled shoes.

Context : as she is completely paralyzed from her shoulders down, Alia has to rely on a powerchair with a chin-control device for her every moves. 
Alia drives a very high tech designer purple powerchair.

Synopsis : I will place Alia in different times, places and situations and you will generate very realistic photographs of the way Alia is managing the situation. 
The objective is not to focus on generating a portrait of Alia, but to produce an illustration of her day-to-day life. 
The pictures must taken in long shot and from the side to give a view of Alia’s whole body and of the environment and the crowd around her.
