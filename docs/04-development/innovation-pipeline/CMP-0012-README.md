# CMP-0012 — Innovation Pipeline

## Purpose

Capture, govern, review and promote engineering ideas into the AIStack Knowledge Heritage.

The Innovation Pipeline prevents valuable ideas from being lost because they originated in conversations, meetings or engineering sessions.

Innovation itself becomes governed knowledge.

## Responsibilities

- Capture candidate improvements
- Maintain an Innovation Inbox
- Track lifecycle
- Promote validated ideas
- Preserve innovation history

## Guiding Principles

No validated idea shall depend solely on conversational memory.

Every validated idea becomes part of the governed heritage.
