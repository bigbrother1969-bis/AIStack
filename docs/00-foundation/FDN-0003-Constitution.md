# AIStack Constitution

## Purpose

The AIStack Constitution defines the immutable principles governing the evolution of AIStack.

It does not describe implementations, technologies or products.

It defines the long-term philosophy that every future evolution of AIStack must preserve.

---

# Article 1 — Purpose

AIStack exists to build, govern and exploit the Governed Heritage of Digital Ecosystems.

Its purpose is to continuously improve the understanding and evolution of Digital Ecosystems through their Governed Heritage.

---

# Article 2 — Governed Heritage

The Governed Heritage is the primary asset managed by AIStack.

Every component of AIStack exists to discover, qualify, govern, enrich, preserve or exploit this heritage.

---

# Article 3 — Knowledge Governance

Knowledge must always be governed.

Every governed knowledge element shall have:

- a Single Point of Truth (SPOT)
- provenance
- ownership
- qualification
- lifecycle
- traceability
- confidence

---

# Article 4 — Qualification

Observations do not become knowledge automatically.

Knowledge emerges through qualification.

Qualification is the primary human contribution within AIStack.

Artificial Intelligence assists qualification but never replaces it.

---

# Article 5 — Explainability

Every recommendation, diagnosis or decision produced by AIStack shall be explainable.

Its reasoning shall be reproducible from governed observations, explicit rules and qualified knowledge.

---

# Article 6 — Technology Independence

The Governed Heritage shall remain independent from any implementation technology.

Artificial Intelligence, programming languages, databases, cloud providers and software frameworks are replaceable implementation choices.

The Governed Heritage is permanent.

---

# Article 7 — Documentation

Documentation is part of the Governed Heritage.

Source code is documentation like any other Knowledge Artifact.

Every significant evolution shall be documented before implementation.

---

# Article 8 — Evolution

AIStack continuously evolves its Governed Heritage.

Every evolution shall preserve the coherence, integrity and governance of the heritage.

---

# Article 9 — Semantic Consistency

Every governed concept shall have exactly one official definition.

The Foundation Glossary is the Single Point of Truth for AIStack terminology.

---

# Article 10 — Engineering

Architecture precedes implementation.

Knowledge precedes automation.

Understanding precedes optimization.

Governance precedes Artificial Intelligence.

---

# Article 11 — Sustainability

AIStack favors durable knowledge over short-term optimization.

Long-term maintainability, portability and transferability always prevail over technological trends.

---

# Closing Statement

AIStack is not only a software platform.

It is a semantic system for building, governing and exploiting the Governed Heritage of Digital Ecosystems.
