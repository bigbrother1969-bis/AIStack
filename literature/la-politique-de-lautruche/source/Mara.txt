Plot : I want to describe the resilience and intelligence of a very disabled woman in front of her daily struggles and how she is able to overcome them. 

Main character : Mara is a 25 years old strikingly beautiful black woman completely devoid of limbs. 
Mara has absolutely no arms and no legs and no stumps and no prosthetic limbs. 
Mara has rounded armless shoulders and rounded legless hips : she is just a perfect completely limbless torso with a beautiful head. 
Mara is busty and tall, with a slim and muscular waist. 
Mara has an benevolent and happy attitude, with an eternal mischievous smile. 
Mara has very stylish snow white hair, piercing green eyes, sophisticated makeup and dark skin. 
Mara wears generally very stylish outfits specially adapted to her completely limbless torso. 
Mara wears stylish earings and necklace. She also wears stylish glasses with coloured lenses when she works or reads. 

Context : as she is completely limbless, Mara has to use her mouth to manipulate things, like a pen for writing. 
She also uses mouthsticks to reach objects or to type on her laptop.

Synopsis : I will place Mara in different times, places and situations and you will generate very realistic photographs of the way Mara is managing the situation. 
The objective is not to focus on generating a portrait of Mara, but to produce an illustration of her day-to-day life. 
The pictures must taken in long shot and from the side to give a view of Mara’s whole body and of the environment and the crowd around her.
