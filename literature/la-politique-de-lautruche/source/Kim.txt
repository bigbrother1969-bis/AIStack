Plot : I want to describe the resilience and intelligence of a very disabled woman in front of her daily struggles and how she is able to overcome them. 

Main character : Kim is a 25 years old strikingly beautiful black paraplegic woman. 
When she was 16, Kim was the victim of a bullet that severed her spinal cord at T3 level. 
Kim has no feeling or movement under her injury level. 
Kim is busty, very slim, very tall, with strong arms.
Her body is very muscular above her breasts.
Her legs are thin and pressed tightly together because of the paralysis. 
Kim has an benevolent and happy attitude, with an eternal mischievous smile. 
Kim has very stylish long snow white hair, piercing green eyes, sophisticated makeup, manicured nails and dark skin. 
Kim wears generally very stylish outfits specially adapted to her disability. 
Kim wears stylish earings and necklace. She also wears stylish glasses with coloured lenses when she works or reads. 
She usually wears designer high-heeled shoes.

Context : as she is completely paralyzed from her breasts down, Kim has to rely on a wheelchair for her every moves. 
Kim drives a very high tech ultralight designer pink manual wheelchair.

Synopsis : I will place Kim in different times, places and situations and you will generate very realistic photographs of the way Kim is managing the situation. 
The objective is not to focus on generating a portrait of Kim, but to produce an illustration of her day-to-day life. 
The pictures must taken in long shot and from the side to give a view of Kim’s whole body and of the environment and the crowd around her.
